The Good News

Jesus said, “I am the bread of life” (John 6:35). God made you for Himself. Our sin separates us from Him, but Jesus died and rose again so that all who trust Him are forgiven and have eternal life.

  • God loves you (Genesis 1:27)
  • Sin separates us (Romans 3:23)
  • Jesus saves (1 Cor 15:3–4)
  • Respond in faith (Romans 10:9–10)
“Jesus said to him, I am the way, and the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me.” — John 14:6

How to Be Saved

Salvation is a gift from God through Jesus Christ. Here’s how you can respond:

  1. Believe — Trust that Jesus died for your sins and rose again (John 3:16).
  2. Confess — Acknowledge your sins before God and ask for forgiveness (Romans 10:9).
  3. Repent — Turn from sin and choose to follow Christ (Acts 3:19).
  4. Receive — Invite Jesus into your life as Lord and Savior (Revelation 3:20).
“If you declare with your mouth, ‘Jesus is Lord,’ and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.” — Romans 10:9

You can pray: “Jesus, I believe You died for my sins and rose again. Forgive me, make me new, and help me follow You. Amen.”

Next Steps

Have you decided to follow Jesus or want to grow deeper? Here are some practical next steps:

1. Pray

Talk with God daily. Share your heart and listen to His Word.

2. Read the Bible

Begin with the Gospel of John or Mark. Read a little each day.

3. Find Community

Join a Bible-believing church or small group for support and growth. Find a church near you →

4. Be Baptized

Take the step of baptism as a public declaration of your faith.

BreadWay.org

BreadWay.org exists to share the Good News of Jesus and help others come to Christ through a simple, recognizable symbol—the bread. When people visit this site, they can find a clear, simple message of hope in Jesus. If you’d like to help spread the Good News, consider placing a sticker on your car or window, or a sign in your yard, business, or church. Every purchase helps keep this site online, funds the creation of stickers and signs, and helps distribute them to more people.
